# Developer Marketing Notes

Key observations when marketing to developer audiences:

## What matters

- Clear value, not hype
- Minimal abstraction
- Control and flexibility
- Speed to implementation
- Real use cases over features

## What doesn't work

- Overly polished marketing language
- Vague benefits
- Hidden complexity
- Forced funnels

## Effective approach

- Show, don't tell (examples, code, demos)
- Reduce friction to first use
- Keep messaging close to the product
- Align marketing with real developer workflows

## Personal focus

I focus on translating technical capabilities into clear, usable value without losing accuracy or oversimplifying the product.
