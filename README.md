# AI Marketing Systems

This repository contains experiments, frameworks, and workflows where I combine AI tools and lightweight code to improve marketing execution.

My focus is on building systems that connect research, messaging, launches, and campaign execution in a scalable and repeatable way.

---

## What I Work On

- AI-assisted market research and competitor analysis  
- Messaging and GTM framework development  
- Product launch systems and multi-team coordination  
- Campaign generation workflows  
- Marketing automation and lightweight scripting  

---

## Example Applications

### Market Research Automation
Using LLMs to analyze competitors, positioning, and messaging patterns at scale.

### Campaign Generation Systems
Structured workflows to generate email sequences, GTM strategies, and content angles using AI.

### Launch Systems
Designing repeatable frameworks to support product launches across marketing, product, and support teams.

---

## Approach

I approach marketing as a system rather than a set of one-off campaigns.

The goal is to reduce manual work, improve consistency, and increase speed to execution by combining:

- AI workflows  
- structured processes  
- lightweight automation  
- cross-functional alignment  

---

## Tools & Stack

- ChatGPT / Claude / Gemini  
- JavaScript (basic utilities)  
- Automation workflows (Zapier, n8n concepts)  
- Funnel and CRM platforms  

---

## Current Focus

Exploring how AI can be used to bridge product, marketing, and growth by creating systems that scale without increasing operational complexity.
