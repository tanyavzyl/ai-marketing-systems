# AI Research Workflow

This is a simple workflow I use to automate market and competitor research using AI tools.

## Steps

1. Define the target company or market
2. Use LLMs to extract:
   - positioning
   - competitors
   - messaging patterns
3. Structure outputs into reusable formats
4. Feed insights into GTM messaging and campaigns

## Goal

Reduce manual research time and create consistent, high-quality inputs for marketing systems.
